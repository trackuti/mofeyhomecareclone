About Us

Mofey Home Care caregivers provide quality home care services for the elderly in Indiana and the surrounding areas.

As part of the exceptional services we offer, we strive to achieve a healthy, safe work environment while supporting the integrity and livelihood of the individuals we serve.

OUR VALUES

Caring

We offer our clients a caring arm and companionship to help them stay as comfortable as possible at home while receiving the care they need.

Integrity

Mofey Home Care follows a strict code of ethics and ensures all caregivers receive adequate ethics in order to provide the best quality care.

Mutual Respect

All members of the Mofey Home Care team treat clients and coworkers with dignity and respect. This mutual respect makes it possible to provide the highest quality care to our clients.
